Welcome to our Website

Swarnandhra College of Engineering & Technology (SCET) was established in 2001 at Narasapuram West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh, India, by the Vasista Educational Society. Gradually, the college has extended many courses like Polytechnic (Diploma), MCA, MBA and M. Tech. The campus is widespread over 25.06 acres located at Seetharampuram on NH-214A, 5 km from Narsapur Town.
